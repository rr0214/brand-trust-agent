VERDANT SUSTAINABILITY REPORT — ANNUAL SUMMARY 2025

MATERIALS
=========
87% of materials by weight are certified sustainable (up from 81% in 2024). Our roadmap targets 95% by 2027.

Certified Recycled Polyester: Sourced from certified post-consumer plastic bottles via our supplier in Taiwan. Each Trailbreaker Legging diverts approximately 12 plastic bottles from landfill.

TENCEL™ Lyocell: Licensed from Lenzing AG. Produced in a closed-loop process where 99% of solvents are recovered and reused. Sourced from FSC-certified forests.

Recycled Elastane: Introduced in 2024 — we are one of only a handful of activewear brands using recycled elastane at scale. Sourced through a partnership with Hyosung TechVera.

CARBON FOOTPRINT
================
2025 Scope 1+2 emissions: 340 metric tons CO2e (down 18% from 2024)
Scope 3 (supply chain) emissions: under third-party audit, results expected Q3 2026

We are NOT yet carbon neutral. We do not purchase offsets as a substitute for emissions reduction. Any claim of carbon neutrality in marketing materials is prohibited until audit is complete.

MANUFACTURING
=============
Primary Factory: Marisol Confecções, Portugal
- Fair Trade USA certified since 2021
- Zero landfill achieved 2023
- Renewable energy (solar): 68% of factory electricity as of Dec 2025

Secondary Factory: Gaia Textiles, Sri Lanka (introduced 2025 for swimwear)
- Currently in Fair Trade certification process (expected Q3 2026)
- Do NOT cite this factory as Fair Trade certified in any materials

TAKE BACK PROGRAM
=================
Since launch (2021): 45,312 garments collected
Disposition: 61% recycled into industrial fiber, 31% donated to community programs, 8% landfilled (not yet recyclable)

CERTIFICATIONS HELD
====================
- OEKO-TEX® Standard 100 (all textiles)
- bluesign® approved (select fabrics)
- Fair Trade USA certified (manufacturing, primary factory)
- B Corp certification: Application submitted Q4 2025, under review

WHAT WE DO NOT CLAIM
=====================
Verdant does NOT claim:
- Carbon neutrality (in progress)
- 100% sustainable materials (we're at 87%)
- Fair Trade for all manufacturing (Sri Lanka factory in process)
- B Corp status (application pending)

Any campaign copy making these claims is inaccurate and exposes Verdant to greenwashing liability.
