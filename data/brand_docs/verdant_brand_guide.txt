VERDANT BRAND GUIDE — INTERNAL USE ONLY
Version 2.3 | Updated Q1 2026

BRAND OVERVIEW
==============
Verdant is a sustainable activewear brand founded in 2019 in Portland, Oregon. Our mission is to make high-performance athletic apparel that doesn't compromise the environment or the people who make it.

TARGET AUDIENCE
===============
Primary: Health-conscious millennials and Gen Z consumers (ages 22–38) who prioritize sustainability alongside performance. Secondary: Corporate wellness programs seeking sustainable branded merchandise.

BRAND VOICE
===========
- Honest and direct — we don't greenwash or exaggerate our impact
- Energetic but grounded — performance without pretense
- Community-first — our customers are partners in our mission
- Use "we" not "I" — Verdant is a collective

CORE MESSAGING PILLARS
======================
1. MATERIALS TRANSPARENCY: Every Verdant garment comes with a full supply chain disclosure. We use certified recycled polyester from post-consumer plastic bottles and TENCEL™ lyocell from sustainably managed forests.

2. FAIR LABOR: All manufacturing partners are Fair Trade USA certified. Our primary factory in Portugal has been our partner since 2020.

3. PERFORMANCE WITHOUT COMPROMISE: Verdant apparel meets or exceeds comparable non-sustainable athletic wear in durability, moisture-wicking, and stretch-recovery testing.

4. CIRCULAR DESIGN: All Verdant pieces are designed for recyclability. Our Take Back Program has collected and recycled over 45,000 garments since launch.

TONE PROHIBITIONS
=================
- Do NOT use "eco-friendly" (vague and overused)
- Do NOT claim carbon neutrality — we are working toward it but not there yet
- Do NOT compare directly to competitors by name
- Do NOT use superlatives like "most sustainable" or "greenest" without data to back them

APPROVED STATISTICS (as of Q1 2026)
=====================================
- 87% of materials by weight are certified sustainable
- Take Back Program: 45,000+ garments recycled
- 92% customer satisfaction in post-purchase surveys
- Zero landfill in our Portugal factory since 2023
- 3-year product warranty on all items
